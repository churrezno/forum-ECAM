SEMINARS & CONVERSATIONS

NOWMENT

ON CONTEMPORARY EVENTS AND AUDIOVISUAL CREATION

Conferences: The present of interest / The NOWMENT of interest
Reflections on the commitment of audiovisual and culture to contemporary events so that those involved in the development of audiovisual projects stop to think to what extent contemporary production takes responsibility for the present and contributes to the configuration of an imaginary of the future for the current and future generations. A compass and guide to understand the world of the 21st century.
